How to Enable 2FA & Create App Password expand_more
Step 1 - Turn ON 2-Step Verification
  1. Open Google Security Settings
  2. Scroll to "Signing in to Google"
  3. Click 2-Step Verification
  4. Turn it ON
Step 2 - Create Gmail App Password
  1. Go to App Passwords page
  2. Sign in again
  3. Select Mail
  4. Select Other (Custom)
  5. Type: AI Gmail Sender
  6. Click Generate
  7. Copy the 16-digit password
Important Notes:
  • Normal Gmail password will NOT work
  • App Password works even if less-secure apps are blocked
  • You can delete & regenerate anytime
